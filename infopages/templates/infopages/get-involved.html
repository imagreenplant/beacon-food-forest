Help from afar

Donate Money

Your donation is tax-deductible and will be put towards many possible expenses including tools, food for volunteers, procuring plants, building materials, etc. Thank you so much, it helps as we are a volunteer-run organization.

Newsletter

This link will sign you up for our mailing list which provides notice of upcoming work parties, events, as well as the monthly newsletter.

Social

Head over to Facebook and like our page. It helps further our reach and is a great way to become part of the community.

Get your hands dirty

Work Party

Come to a work party and engage in activities to help develop and maintain the Beacon Food Forest. ALL ages and skill levels are welcome. Lunch will be served. Bring your gloves and head over because we want to see you, rain or shine. Orientations for new volunteers are offered at every work party. Check the calendar for the next work party.

Other activities

During summer we have sunset labs, maintenance and harvest of the Helix farm plot, wetland restoration and various other tasks as they come up. Come by a site development meeting (see calendar) or email the volunteer coordinator for more information.

Sign Up for a P-Patch

The Beacon Food Forest is one giant P-Patch (garden). Sign up to tend your own plot. Volunteer hours as well as a small fee are required to maintain a P-Patch. Sign up is done through the city of Seattle.

Get even more involved

Attend an orientation

Want to learn the workings of the Beacon Food Forest and help out the organization? Writing grants, holding classes, leading tasks, volunteer coordination, or simply just being a part of an amazing community of volunteers who do their best. ALL skill levels are welcome. Check the calendar for the next new volunteer orientation.

Email the volunteer coordinator

Our volunteer coordinators are here to help. Don't know where to start? Send an email and start asking questions.

Art projects

Dream big, and bring your art proposal to our Community Outreach Coordinator. We relish art and that which brings harmony. Get yourself a grant and see that vision come to life at the Beacon Food Forest!

Get your fellow humans involved

Setup a group work party

We invite organizations or large groups to come help on site. Email our group visits coordinator to get matched up with some volunteers that will help coordinate tasks and planning for the big day out of the office.

Tour

The Beacon Food Forest is global. People from Taiwan, Ukraine, France and many others have visited to understand just how the magic happened here. Free tours are offered during our monthly work parties. See the calendar for dates. If you are a big group and would like your own tour, email the group visits coordinator. Kindly consider making a donation to support our volunteer-run organization.
