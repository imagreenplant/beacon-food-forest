{% block title %}Code of Conduct{% endblock title %}

At the Beacon Food Forest​, we strive to actively create an inclusive and compassionate community.

By joining any of our classes, gatherings or events, you agree to help maintain a ​safe and welcoming space for all​, ​by adhering to the P-Patch Code of Conduct.

Discriminatory or threatening behaviors can be reported to the work party leader or class facilitator, or directly to our board at board@beaconfoodforest.org.

The Beacon Food Forest is committed to fostering equity, understanding and kindness throughout our community and the world.
