Support the Beacon Food Forest

As we are powered by our community, we appreciate and welcome any donations for you to show your support of the Beacon Food Forest.

Donate through GROW, our fiscal sponsor

For your contribution to be recognized as a tax deductible donation, please make your check out to our fiscal sponsor GROW. In the memo line on the check include our legal name: Food Forest Collective.

Mail the check to:

GROW
PO Box 19748
Seattle, WA 98109

For other donations or concerns, please email donate@beaconfoodforest.org

Your donation will be put towards many possible expenses including tools, food for volunteers, procuring plants, building materials, etc. Thank you so much, it helps as we are a volunteer-run organization.
