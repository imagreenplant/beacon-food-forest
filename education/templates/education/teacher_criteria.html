CRITERIA FOR PROPOSING A WORKSHOP

Instructor Criteria

  • Volunteers of the friends of Beacon Food Forest
    • An Instructor who is prepared and knowledgeable in a topic, and has demonstrated knowledge and communication skills to the BFF team
    • A new instructor whose abilities and knowledge are unknown to the PEC team, and who is willing to conduct a sample workshop at a BFF work party to demonstrate their knowledge and abilities.
  • People who have contributed to the permaculture knowledge bank and will contribute to BFF growth and management

Workshop Topic Criteria

  • permaculture-related topics
  • topics which meet the mission of the BFF
